# Research & Innovation

Advanced research in next-generation wireless communication systems, intelligent networks, and spectrum-efficient technologies.


---

 
## Research Areas

### 5G Use Cases
Practical and emerging applications of 5G including industry, smart infrastructure, and mission-critical systems.

### 5G Security
Security, privacy, and resilience of 5G RAN, Core, and network slicing architectures.

### 5G Enabled Drones
UAV communication systems integrated with 5G and Beyond-5G networks for control, telemetry, and edge intelligence.

### AI for Wireless
Machine learning and deep learning for PHY, MAC, spectrum sensing, and network optimization.

### Spectrum Management
Efficient spectrum utilization using cognitive radio, dynamic access, and intelligent allocation.

### IoT Security
Security frameworks and intrusion detection for large-scale IoT and machine-type communication networks.
