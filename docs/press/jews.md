# JEWS – Journal of Experimental Wireless Systems

**JEWS** is a peer‑reviewed journal published by
**Basig Wireless Press**, focusing on cutting‑edge research in
wireless communication systems.

## Scope
- 5G / 6G Networks
- Software Defined Radio (SDR)
- AI / ML for Wireless
- IoT and Edge Computing
- Spectrum Management and Open RAN

JEWS aims to bridge **theory, experimentation, and real‑world deployment**.

Access the official journal website for publications, scope, and submissions.  
🔗 [https://jewsjournal.org](https://jewsjournal.org)
